Lexatron is a turn-based word race!

Form words using your letters starting from one corner of the board. Reach the
opposite side first and win! Get blocked by your opponent's letters? Blow them
up with a bomb and keep going!

• turn-based, asynchronous gameplay
• online 2-player multiplayer
• random match-ups 
• challenge your friends (contacts, Facebook)
• pass-and-play matches
• in-game chat
• multiple random bombs per match
• up to 25 simultaneous matches
